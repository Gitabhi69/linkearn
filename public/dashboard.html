Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I use my API Key?

Your API key is a unique code that links your account to our Telegram bot. Go to the API tab, generate your key, and send it to the @HelpEarnRobot. You only need to do this once.

What is the withdrawal process?

First, save your USDT BEP20 wallet address in the "Payment" section. Once your balance reaches the minimum amount of $100 USD, you can request a withdrawal. Requests are typically processed within 24-48 hours.

How are my earnings calculated?

Your earnings are calculated based on the number of unique views on your links. The rate can vary. Your dashboard balance updates in real-time, while daily revenue is calculated based on Indian Standard Time (IST).

Why can't I edit my email address?

For security reasons, your email address is tied to your account and cannot be changed from the dashboard. If you need to update your email, please contact our support team.
