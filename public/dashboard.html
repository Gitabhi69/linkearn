Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I use my API Key?

Your API key is a unique code that links your account to our Telegram bot. Go to the API tab, generate your key, and send it to the @HelpEarnRobot bot. You only need to do this once.

What is the withdrawal process?

First, save your USDT BEP20 wallet address in the "Payment" section. Once your balance reaches the minimum amount of $100 USD, you can request a withdrawal.

Why can't I edit my email address?

For security reasons, your email is tied to your account and cannot be changed here. Please contact support for assistance.
